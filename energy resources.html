5. Benefits and Disadvantages of Energy Sources

Energy Source Benefits Disadvantages
Coal Abundant, reliable, high energy density Polluting, contributes to global warming, non-renewable
Oil Transportable, versatile, high energy output Spills, finite supply, geopolitical tensions
Natural Gas Cleaner than coal, efficient Methane emissions, pipeline costs, non-renewable
Solar Unlimited source, no emissions Weather-dependent, high storage costs, may not generate enough energy
Wind Renewable, no emissions Intermittent, noise pollution, Weather-dependent
Nuclear Low emissions, high output Risk of accidents, waste disposal
